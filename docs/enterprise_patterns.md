# Enterprise AI Patterns

## Pattern 1: AI as a Service Layer

AI sits between users and enterprise systems, providing intelligent processing.

## Pattern 2: RAG + Agent

Agents retrieve relevant data (RAG) and use it to generate structured outputs.

## Pattern 3: AI as Decision Engine

AI produces structured decisions that can drive downstream systems.

## Pattern 4: Tool + Context Hybrid

AI systems combine tools (APIs/functions) and context (documents/data).

## Pattern 5: Human-in-the-Loop

AI systems should include human validation for critical workflows.

## In My AI Lab

## Key Insight

Enterprise AI systems are not just models — they are integrated systems combining data, tools, workflows, and human oversight.
